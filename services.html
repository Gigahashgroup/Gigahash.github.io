Our Services

DLE Technology Sourcing

We maintain a global network of leading Direct Lithium Extraction (DLE) providers and match the most suitable technologies to each project’s brine chemistry, location and scale. Our team conducts due diligence on recovery efficiency, reagent consumption, and ESG performance to ensure technologies meet international standards.

Once a technology is selected, we help negotiate terms and integrate the solution into your development plan, ensuring compatibility with existing infrastructure and downstream processing.

Project De‑Risking

Successful lithium projects require more than technology. We undertake comprehensive brine resource assessments, including sampling, lab testing and hydrogeological modelling. Our experts evaluate infrastructure availability, power and water requirements, and outline regulatory pathways for licensing and export permits.

By identifying and mitigating potential obstacles early, we improve the bankability of projects, reduce timeline uncertainty and enhance investor confidence.

Smart Contract Partnership Structuring

Gigahash Global structures partnerships on transparent smart contracts deployed on blockchain. These contracts govern capital contributions, milestones and automated revenue distribution, ensuring every party’s rights and obligations are immutably encoded.

We coordinate legal review and help customise contract parameters to reflect project‑specific terms, aligning all partners’ incentives and reducing counter‑party risk.

Brine Resource Assessment

Our geoscientists perform in‑depth studies of brine reservoirs, including chemical characterisation, reservoir modelling and pilot extraction tests. This data forms the foundation for sizing DLE equipment and forecasting production volumes.

Combined with financial modelling, brine assessment helps determine project viability and informs partnership terms.

Regulatory Compliance

We navigate complex regulatory frameworks across African jurisdictions, helping clients obtain exploration licences, environmental approvals and export permits. Our team liaises with authorities to ensure projects comply with local and international ESG standards.

We also monitor evolving policy landscapes to keep projects aligned with future reporting requirements and cross‑border trade rules.

Market Development

Beyond extraction, Gigahash Global assists in positioning produced lithium products for premium markets. We facilitate offtake agreements, advise on tolling and refining options, and support marketing of tokenised revenue streams when appropriate.

Our market intelligence helps partners maximise returns while maintaining transparency and traceability from resource to end‑product.
