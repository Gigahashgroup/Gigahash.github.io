Implementation Roadmap

Phase 1: Brine Evaluation

Collect and analyse brine samples, conduct reservoir modelling and assess infrastructure to determine resource quality and development potential.

Phase 2: DLE Integration Pilot

Select suitable direct lithium extraction technologies and perform pilot‑scale tests to validate recovery rates, reagent consumption and scalability.

Phase 3: Smart Contract Structuring

Define partnership terms, investment structures and revenue distribution, then encode them into audited smart contracts on blockchain.

Phase 4: Deployment & Scaling

Construct full‑scale extraction facilities, integrate DLE modules, secure permitting and commence commercial production with ongoing technical support.

Phase 5: Long‑Term Monitoring

Operate production with continuous monitoring of brine chemistry, ESG metrics and smart contract performance. Adjust processes and reinvest to optimise returns.
