Commercial & Financing

Smart Contract Financing

Gigahash Global structures financing rounds as smart contracts that allocate investor funds to specific project milestones. Each drawdown is triggered by verified completion of geologic surveys, pilot tests or construction phases. This ensures capital is deployed efficiently and transparently.

Automated Revenue Distribution

Revenue from lithium sales is tokenised and distributed automatically to stakeholders according to their share in the smart contract. Payment waterfalls are coded to prioritise debt service, return of capital and profit sharing. Investors receive real‑time reporting and on‑chain audit trails.

Investor Protection

Our financing model includes escrow accounts, performance bonds and insurance mechanisms to mitigate operational and market risks. Smart contracts can pause distributions if compliance metrics fall below thresholds, ensuring capital preservation.

Cross‑Border Compliance

We design financing structures that comply with local and international regulations, including currency controls, anti‑money laundering rules and securities laws. Tokenised instruments are issued in accordance with jurisdictional requirements to enable cross‑border investment.

Royalty & Equity Structures

Depending on partner preferences, commercial terms may include streaming, royalty or equity stakes. Smart contracts reflect these structures and automatically calculate payouts based on production volumes and pricing benchmarks.
