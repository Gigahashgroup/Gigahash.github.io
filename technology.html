Technology & Integration

DLE Integration

We work with modular, high‑recovery direct lithium extraction systems that deliver >92 % lithium recovery rates from complex brine chemistries. Our integration process assesses brine composition, temperature and flow to configure adsorption, solvent‑extraction or ion‑exchange modules for optimal performance.

Once selected, we oversee pilot trials, evaluate reagent regeneration and ensure compatibility with downstream crystallisation or conversion circuits.

Smart Contract Enforcement

All project agreements are encoded into smart contracts on a secure blockchain. These digital agreements automatically execute fund draws, milestone payments and revenue distribution based on pre‑defined triggers such as production volumes or pricing benchmarks.

This eliminates manual reconciliation and reduces counterparty risk while providing immutable audit trails for all stakeholders.

Immutable Transparency

Our platform leverages blockchain’s immutable ledger to record production data, ESG metrics and financial transactions. Each brine batch, extraction run and shipment can be traced from source to end‑use, enabling transparent reporting to regulators, investors and offtakers.

Immutable records build trust among partners and unlock access to tokenised financing instruments.

ESG & Compliance Mechanics

We encode environmental and social governance metrics into smart contracts, ensuring adherence to local and international standards. Emission intensity, water consumption and community impact indicators can be linked to financing terms, incentivising sustainable operation.

Compliance reports are available in real time to investors and regulators, demonstrating responsible resource development.

Blockchain Architecture Overview

The Gigahash platform runs on a permissioned blockchain with layer‑2 scalability. Smart contracts are deployed using audited templates, while oracles feed external data such as price indexes and production sensors. Role‑based access controls ensure data confidentiality among partners.
