Partnership Opportunities

Gigahash Global forms collaborative partnerships to unlock value across the lithium supply chain. We categorise partners to ensure clear protections and value propositions for each group.

Technology Providers

We partner with innovative DLE and processing technology companies seeking to commercialise their solutions in Africa. Through smart contracts, technology IP is protected, usage rights are clearly defined and royalty streams are automated.

  • Protection mechanisms: IP escrow on blockchain, milestone‑based licensing.
  • Smart contract assurances: automated royalty payments, usage tracking.
  • Partner gains: access to brine resources, accelerated adoption and transparent revenue sharing.

Funding Partners

Capital providers—including equity investors, lenders and royalty funds—participate in projects through tokenised financing instruments. Smart contracts govern capital deployment, drawdowns and repayment schedules tied to production metrics.

  • Protection mechanisms: escrowed funds, waterfall payment structures.
  • Smart contract assurances: immutable record of investment terms and automated distributions.
  • Partner gains: exposure to lithium projects with transparent risk controls and regular payouts.

Brine Resource Suppliers

Resource owners contribute brine concessions in exchange for technology access and development capital. Our frameworks ensure their mineral rights are protected while unlocking value through joint ventures.

  • Protection mechanisms: title verification on chain, pre‑defined dilution terms.
  • Smart contract assurances: clear participation percentages, automated revenue allocation.
  • Partner gains: accelerated project development, technology transfer and transparent revenue share.
